Why this role exists

This is not a reporting role. It's a building role.

We're a profitable, founder-led D2C company running multiple brands across Amazon, Shopify, and local marketplaces in Europe, the UK, and the US. We have around 50 people across three continents. The data is there. The dashboards exist. What we're missing is someone who can make them mean something.

We need a commercially-minded finance person who doesn't work in a silo — someone who sits with Brand Managers, digs into the Supply Chain numbers, and turns operational decisions into financial insight before they become expensive mistakes. This is a build-from-scratch role, not a maintain-the-status-quo one. You'll work directly with the CEO and COO from day one.

Three pillars. All of them matter from the start.

Drive the annual business plan across departments, own variance analysis, and give leadership real-time visibility into cash flow, liquidity, and capital efficiency. No more guesswork from static spreadsheets.

02

What you'll do

The work — in detail.

Business Planning & Reporting

  • Drive the annual business plan, keeping Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Management aligned on assumptions, targets, and risks
  • Lead the budgeting cycle, own variance analysis, and deliver clear monthly insight into actual vs. forecast
  • Translate operational decisions — product launches, sourcing, expansion — into their impact on liquidity and profitability
  • Automate KPI dashboards and replace manual spreadsheet processes with scalable systems
  • Build a single-source-of-truth reporting layer that leadership actually uses

Product Portfolio & Cash Flow

  • Build and maintain product cohort analysis — revenue, margin, and growth contribution by launch year and lifecycle stage
  • Tailor forecasting assumptions by stage: launch, scale-up, maturity, decline — not a one-size-fits-all model
  • Identify cash leaks: slow-moving inventory, unprofitable SKUs, storage fee creep, return-rate margin erosion
  • Build the financial case for product discontinuation — working capital freed up, opportunity cost of keeping them alive
  • Maintain a 2-year liquidity and cash flow plan, surface risks early, and recommend operational adjustments

Cross-Functional Partnership

  • Partner with Brand Managers, Supply Chain, and R&D — finance is the connective tissue, not a back-office function
  • Challenge product, sourcing, and marketing decisions with data — not just report on them
  • Bring portfolio-level financial discipline to launch, reinvestment, and sunset decisions

Tools & AI

  • Leverage AI tools — Claude, LLM-powered analysis — to accelerate financial modeling and insight generation
  • Drive integration of finance tools with existing systems where it improves speed or quality of insight
  • We're an AI-forward company. We expect you to use modern tools, not tolerate them

Job description · Open role

Senior Creatives Associate — own the visual design behind our DACH-level Amazon listings.

We're looking for a Senior Creatives Associate to take ownership of the visual design behind our Amazon listings, with a strong focus on the DACH market — one of the most demanding and conversion-driven design standards in e-commerce.

Full-time · Permanent Remote · Open (design quality over origin) Reports to Creative Director Creative Team
Apply now
About the role

This is not a purely artistic role.

This is a grow-into role: you'll start with focused execution and rework of existing listings, and grow over time into full creative ownership of image conception, storyline, and carousel logic — with the Creative Director providing final approval.

You'll need a genuine understanding of conversion-oriented design — how layout, imagery, and messaging influence click-through and conversion, not just how something looks.

The German-speaking Amazon market expects a high visual standard — more demanding and more customer-oriented than most other marketplaces. What matters is the ability to deliver that design quality and understand the German buyer's expectations around perceived value, credibility, and clarity — not where you're based or your native language.

What you'll do

Key responsibilities.

Primary focus at entry

Rework of Existing Products

  • Visually upgrade and modernize existing listing images and A+ Content
  • Revise outdated designs and adjust imagery when product scope or specs change
  • Make targeted image adjustments in response to negative customer reviews
  • Implement reworks based on Brand Manager specifications
  • Provide functional guidance to 3D/rendering designers on rework projects — creating tasks and requesting images as needed
Execution growing into conception

Launch Products

  • Execute image design based on creative direction and layout provided by the Creative Director (graphic elements, icons, overlays, badges)
  • Handle text and element placement, carousel consistency, and A+ Content graphics
  • Perform retouching: finishing, cutouts, shadows, lighting, and detail cleanup before final approval

Additional Design Work

  • Print and digital flyers
  • B2B catalogs and sales assets
  • Maintain reusable templates and design building blocks

Collaboration

  • Coordinate with 3D/rendering designers, with collaboration deepening over time from functional guidance toward more independent coordination
  • Work with production support on simpler supporting tasks as needed
  • Final approval of all creative outputs remains with the Creative Director
  • For German-language copy, native-speaker support is provided by the Creative Director for final linguistic refinement
Your growth path

How the role grows over time.

This role is designed to expand in scope and autonomy as trust and track record are established.

1
You start here

Entry

Focus is primarily on rework of existing listings; new launches are executed under close creative direction, with frequent consultation on concept and layout.

2
Next step

Development

Rework is handled fully independently; increasing input on launch concepts, with routine design decisions made independently.

3
Target state

Full creative ownership

Full ownership of visual conception across both rework and launches — including storyline, image build, and conversion logic, plus conversion-oriented image copy — with the Creative Director retaining final approval.
